# Effect of Rainfall Deficit on Surface Water Storage Changes

Case Study of Surface Water Storage Assessment in Bundelkhand, India

------

## Project Overview


A national census of waterbodies revealed a 2.6 cm/year decline in groundwater storage, underscoring the severity of the situation and need for a water storage framework. Water storage framework was developed using satellite images and Google Earth Engine to assess surface water storage across the Ramganga River Basin and the Bundelkhand region (Chhatarpur and Panna districts, Madhya Pradesh) within the wider Ganga Basin.

Surface water mapping established the first digital inventory of 6,500 small water bodies (1–5 hectares) in the region and demonstrated the significance of their intra-annual variability for land and water management during the lean (dry) months — insights critical for irrigation planning, fisheries and livestock, and crop production resilience under a changing climate.
